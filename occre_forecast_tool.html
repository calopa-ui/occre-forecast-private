📊 SKU Stock Status
Column Guide
Current Stock
Units in your warehouse today. Type directly in the cell or import from a file.
Monthly Rate
Average monthly sales. Source set in Settings above (Current Year Actuals or Forecast).
Weeks Coverage
Current Stock ÷ Weekly Rate. How many weeks your stock will last at the current pace.
Status
🚨 Critical <4w ⚠️ Reorder 4–8w ✅ OK 8–16w 📦 Excess >16w
Recommended PO
(Target + Lead Time + Safety − Coverage) × Weekly Rate. Units to order to reach your target. ✓ means no order needed.
⚙️ Settings:   Target = ideal weeks of stock to hold  ·  Lead Time = weeks from placing a PO to receiving goods  ·  Safety Stock = buffer weeks against demand spikes  ·  Reorder trigger = Lead Time + Safety Stock
